# Core D6 Mechanics (Aetherium Tontine Variant)

## The Die Pool
- **Format:** X D + Y (e.g., 5D+2 = roll five six-sided dice, add 2)
- **Attribute Range:** 2D to 5D (starting characters have 18D total)
- **Skill Range:** 0D (untrained) to 7D (master)

## The Wild Die
- **One distinct die per roll:**

  - **1:** Subtract die + highest other die roll (critical complication)
  - **Character Points:** Spend up to 2 per roll for +1D-2D bonus (post-roll declaration)
  - **Fate Points:** Spend 1 pre-roll to double ALL dice (Eureka moment)

## Aetherium Tontine Wild Die Modifiers
- **In Flux 4D+:** Wild Die explodes on 5 or 6
- **In Flux 0D:** Wild Die is disabled (no 6, no 1, just normal die)
- **In Verdant Scar:** Roll 2 Wild Dice (reality is interpretive)

## Opposed Rolls
- Higher total wins; ties favor defender
- **Margin of Success:** For every 5 points over TN, gain +1D on next related action

## Derived Statistics
- **Body Points:** 20 + Strength die code (e.g., 3D+2 Strength = 20+11 = 31 BP)
- **Move:** 10 + Reflexes pips (e.g., 3D+2 = 10+2 = 12 meters/round)
- **Character Points:** 5 + Perception pips (spend for bonus dice, also XP)
- **Fate Points:** 1 + Spirit pips (double dice, pre-roll)

## Mana & Flux
- **Mythic Flux:** Hidden GM stat (0D to 10D) tracking regional magic
- **Repletion:** Actions that raise Flux (narrative-driven)
- **Depletion:** Aetherium use that lowers Flux (mechanical cost)

## GM Warning
If Flux hits 0D, campaign shifts to **Pure Tech-Noir**: no magic, no Wild Die, automatons rule. Plan accordingly.
