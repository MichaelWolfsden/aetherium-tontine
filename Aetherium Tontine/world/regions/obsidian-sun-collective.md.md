# The Obsidian Sun Collective (Meso-American)

## Geography
- **Capital:** Tenochtitlan-Reforged (Pyramid-Refinery City)
- **Climate:** Ash-fall from refineries, blood-heat mirages
- **Dominant Terrain:** Ziggurat-refineries, blood-mana pipelines, slum-mazes
- **Flux Level:** 1D (ravaged, but blood-mana concentrates power)

## Fracture History
- **Year 132-180:** Harvest War—Admiralty's "resource extraction" genocide
- **Year 300:** Tenochtitlan-Reforged built on ashes, blood-mana refinement industrialized
- **Year 411:** Rite of the Smoking Mirror—Tezcatlipoca's Breath weapon created
- **Current Crisis:** Consortium bounty on Jaguar Knights, internal debate over using Breath weapon (could destroy world faster)

## Political Landscape
- **Faction Resistance:** Jaguar Knights lead covert war
- **Resource Control:** 100% of blood-mana, 0% of political autonomy
- **Subversive Elements:** Publicly none—Jaguar Knights operate in absolute secrecy

## Cultural Notes
- **Syncretic Rage:** Circuit-diagram tattoos enchanted with old prayers
- **Technology:** Blood-powered, ritual-hybridized, beautiful and terrible
- **Motto:** *"I will die. My people's magic will not."*

## Current Threats
- **Harvest War II:** Consortium considering "final extraction" of remaining blood-mages
- **Internal Schism:** Some Jaguar Knights want to use Tezcatlipoca's Breath on Vostok-Prime
