# The Iron Compact (Germanic)

## Geography
- **Capital:** Königsforge (Cogwork Citadel)
- **Climate:** Perpetual forge-smoke, mechanized weather control
- **Dominant Terrain:** Factory-mountains, piston-valleys, railway-spines
- **Flux Level:** 0D (fully rationalized)

## Fracture History
- **Year 18:** Guild of Ironwrights unifies Germanic states under "Stahl und Dampf"
- **Year 100:** Ferromancer's Spine construction begins, ley-line stripping methodology
- **Year 156:** First mass-produced automatons roll off assembly lines
- **Year 231:** Logiker calculate depletion curve—seal results as state secret
- **Current Crisis:** Gear-Priest heresy spreading among junior engineers

## Political Landscape
- **Faction Stronghold:** Consortium (tier-1 industrial control)
- **Subversive Elements:** Klagelied (Gear-Priests) operating in hidden chapels, Unchained Protocol infiltrating factories
- **Resource Control:** 90% of gear production, 70% of factory automation

## Cultural Notes
- **Philosophy:** "Brutal efficiency is mercy"
- **Augmentation:** Orcish piston-grafting standard for shock troops
- **Technology:** Mass production, hydraulic pressgangs, automaton legions

## Current Threats
- **Klagelied Heresy:** 15% of engineers suspected of machine-soul worship
- **Automaton Unchaining:** Alpha-7's Underground Railroad using maintenance tunnels
