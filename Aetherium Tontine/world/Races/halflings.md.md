# Halflings (The Garden Society)

## Cultural Adaptation
They were the first miniaturizers, the ones who made Aetherium portable. Their shires were the first to be blightedâ€”turned into monoculture farms for "maximum efficiency." Now they wander in caravans, tending hidden gardens in the shadows of cities. They preserve seeds, songs, and sanity. Their greatest treasure is a **Real Seed** from before the Fracture, said to grow into a tree that produces pure mana fruit.

## D6 Mechanics
- **Attribute Mods:** +1D Coordination, +1D Perception, -1D Strength (small)
- **Advantages:**
  - **Unobtrusive** (-1D): +2D Stealth in urban environments (people don't notice halflings)
  - **Green Thumb** (-2D): Can create Mythic Resonance +0.5D by tending gardens in blight zones
  - **Resistant** (-1D): +1D to resist rationalization sickness
- **Disadvantages:**
  - **Homesick** (+2D): -1D to all rolls when >10 miles from last "garden sanctuary"
  - **Diaspora** (+1D): No homeland, treated as second-class by all empires
  - **Small** (+1D): -1D to Intimidate, -1D to reach high objects
- **Starting Templates:** Garden-Mage, Caravan-Scout, Seed-Preserver

## Current Status
- **Population:** Diaspora (4% of global, scattered)
- **Flux Resilience:** Medium (create pocket-sanctuaries)
- **Social Standing:** Invisible (ignored, useful for "sanitizing" blight zones)

## Caravans
- **Wandering Convocation:** Mayor Thorn's group, largest (+1D Social)
- **Garden-Wayfarers:** Travel between cities, selling Dream-Dust antidotes (+1D Herbalism)
- **Seed-Keepers:** Guardians of the Real Seed, secretive (+1D Stealth)
