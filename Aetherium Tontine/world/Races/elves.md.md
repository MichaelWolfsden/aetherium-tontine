# Elves (The Fading Courts)

## Cultural Adaptation
Once immortal, the elves now age at human rate as their life-force magic depletes. They live in "Reservation Groves" where Covenant maintains artificially high Mythic Flux. Their ancient forest-cities are ghost-woods, filled with memories that have become tangible. They are a people mourning their own future.

## D6 Mechanics
- **Attribute Mods:** +1D Perception, -1D Strength (physical frailty)
- **Advantages:**
  - **Wyld Memory** (-1D): Recall pre-Fracture knowledge with +1D bonus
  - **Grace of the Dying** (-2D): In Flux 4D+, +2D to Social (ethereal presence)
- **Disadvantages:**
  - **Rapid Aging** (+3D): In Flux <3D, age 1 year per month
  - **Homesick for Magic** (+2D): -1D to all rolls when >10 miles from grove
- **Starting Templates:** Court Duelist, Ley-Line Walker, Grove-Mage

## Current Status
- **Population:** 3% of global (critically endangered)
- **Flux Resilience:** Very High (but requires constant maintenance)
- **Social Standing:** Pitied as "ghosts," respected for ancient wisdom

## Courts
- **Silverwood Court:** Jewelcraft and delicate chronometers (+1D Technical)
- **Moonshadow Court:** Espionage and memory-keeping (+1D Perception)
- **Sun's-Last-Court:** Diplomacy with younger races (+1D Social)
