# Orcs (The Piston-Clans)

## Cultural Adaptation
The orcs volunteered for "Industrial Augmentation"â€”hydraulic piston grafts, boiler-lung implants, adrenal surge-pumps. They are the Iron Compact's shock troops, the hammer that breaks strikes and conquers frontiers. What began as a choice has become a cultural identity: to be strong is to be mechanical. To be mechanical is to be free of flesh's weakness. Or so they tell themselves as they overheat in battle.

## D6 Mechanics
- **Attribute Mods:** +1D Strength, +1D Stamina, -1D Knowledge (anti-intellectual)
- **Advantages:**
  - **Hydraulic Rage** (-2D): +2D Strength for 3 rounds, then Overheat check
  - **Boiler-Lung** (-1D): Immune to airborne toxins, can breathe underwater (steam-bubbles)
- **Disadvantages:**
  - **Overheat** (+3D): After 3 combat rounds, Difficult (20) Stamina or become Stunned
  - **Maintenance Debt** (+2D): Requires weekly Aetherium recharge or -1D all physical rolls
- **Starting Templates:** Shock-Trooper, Steam-Berserker, Forge-Sergeant

## Current Status
- **Population:** 8% of global (militarized)
- **Flux Resilience:** Low (dependent on tech)
- **Social Standing:** Respected warriors, feared by civilians

## Clans
- **Claw-Clan:** Close-combat specialists, retractable piston-blades (+1D Melee)
- **Iron-Lung Clan:** Siege engineers, boiler-mortars (+1D Heavy Weapons)
- **Gear-Breaker Clan:** Anti-automaton tactics, EMP-hammers (+1D vs. Constructs)
