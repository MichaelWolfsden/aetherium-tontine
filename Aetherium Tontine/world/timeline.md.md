# The Fracture Era: 500 Years of Depletion
*A chronological record of wonder's death*

## **YEAR -3: The Last Prophecy** (Pre-Fracture)
- **Event:** Scandinavian Völva prophesies the Fracture
- **Region:** Raven Throne
- **Significance:** Last accurate prophecy in recorded history
- **People:** Magic still wild, gods still speak
- **Flux Level:** 10D (pre-industrial baseline)

## **YEAR 0: The Sunderer's Gambit**
- **Event:** Archimedes of New Byzantium distills first Aetherium rod from ley line nexus
- **Region:** Obsidian Senate (New Byzantium)
- **Significance:** Birth of the Tontine. Magic becomes fuel.
- **Mechanics:** First conversion of mana to Aetherium (0.0001% efficiency)
- **Deltas:** [d1::] world/regions/obsidian-senate.md → Flux Level 10D → 9D

## **YEAR 12: The Admiralty's Sin**
- **Event:** Queen Octavia I charters Crown Aetherium Company
- **Region:** Aetheric Admiralty
- **Significance:** Magic industrialized. Maritime-to-sky conversion begins.
- **Mechanics:** First sky-fleet construction protocols
- **Deltas:** [d2::] world/regions/aetheric-admiralty.md → Aetherium extraction begins

## **YEAR 18: The Iron Compact**
- **Event:** Guild of Ironwrights unifies Germanic states under "Stahl und Dampf"
- **Region:** Iron Compact
- **Significance:** Mass production begins. Ley lines mapped for stripping.
- **Mechanics:** Ferromancer's Spine construction initiated
- **Deltas:** [d3::] world/regions/iron-compact.md → Guild coalition formed

## **YEAR 45: Senate Schism**
- **Event:** Obsidian Senate civil war—Optimates (pro-Aetherium) vs. Populares (pro-magic)
- **Region:** Obsidian Senate
- **Significance:** Populares lose, go underground → Philosopher's Crypt founded
- **Mechanics:** First political resistance to Aetherium
- **Deltas:** [d4::] world/factions/covenant.md → Crypt becomes shadow government

## **YEAR 50: Sky Dominion**
- **Event:** Admiralty has more airships than sea-ships
- **Region:** Aetheric Admiralty
- **Significance:** Air superiority established, sky-trade monopoly begins
- **Mechanics:** Aetheric turbines replace sails
- **Deltas:** [d5::] world/regions/aetheric-admiralty.md → Become sky power

## **YEAR 67: The Crypt's Gambit**
- **Event:** Philosopher's Crypt becomes Covenant bank and shadow government
- **Region:** Obsidian Senate
- **Significance:** Covenant gains funding, Senate becomes dual-layered
- **Mechanics:** Blood-mana money laundering through steamship futures
- **Deltas:** [d6::] world/factions/covenant.md → Financial infrastructure established

## **YEAR 87: The Sultanate's Pact**
- **Event:** Dusk Sultanate performs Rite of Last Flowers—sacrifices magical potency for survival
- **Region:** Dusk Sultanate
- **Significance:** First sustainable alternative to Aetherium stripping
- **Mechanics:** Resonance well creation methodology
- **Deltas:** [d7::] world/regions/dusk-sultanate.md → Flux Level 10D → 7D (sacrifice)

## **YEAR 89: The Raven's Defiance**
- **Event:** Raven King burns longships rather than convert to Aetherium
- **Region:** Raven Throne
- **Significance:** Last stand against Aetherium; hybrid power philosophy born
- **Mechanics:** "Riding the Wave and the Gear" guerrilla tactics
- **Deltas:** [d8::] world/regions/raven-throne.md → Resistance movement begins

## **YEAR 100: The Spine Begins**
- **Event:** Ferromancer's Spine construction starts, ley-line stripping methodology perfected
- **Region:** Iron Compact
- **Significance:** Systematic depletion of world's magic infrastructure
- **Mechanics:** 1,000-mile railway following dead ley lines
- **Deltas:** [d9::] world/locations/ferromancer-spine.md → Construction phase

## **YEAR 101: The Mandate's Answer**
- **Event:** Jade Mandate unveils Thousand-Jade Engine after century of study
- **Region:** Jade Mandate
- **Significance:** First sustainable Aetherium burn (1/10th mythic cost)
- **Mechanics:** Resonance Harmony philosophy
- **Deltas:** [d10::] world/regions/jade-mandate.md → Flux Level 10D → 9D (controlled burn)

## **YEAR 132: The Harvest War Begins**
- **Event:** Admiralty "exploratory fleet" discovers Meso-American empires; sees reservoirs, not people
- **Region:** Obsidian Sun Collective
- **Significance:** Blood-mana extraction industrialized; genocide begins
- **Mechanics:** Blood-mana = 10x potency of standard Aetherium
- **Deltas:** [d11::] world/regions/obsidian-sun-collective.md → Occupation begins, Flux 10D → 3D

## **YEAR 156: The Golem Factories**
- **Event:** Iron Compact first to mass-produce automatons
- **Region:** Iron Compact
- **Significance:** Labor revolution (and exploitation)
- **Mechanics:** Automaton assembly lines operational
- **Deltas:** [d12::] world/factions/consortium.md → Automaton legions available

## **YEAR 180: The Harvest War Ends**
- **Event:** Tenochtitlan "pacified," blood-mana pipeline operational
- **Region:** Obsidian Sun Collective
- **Significance:** 50,000 blood-mages dead, Emperor puppeted, Collective becomes refinery-state
- **Mechanics:** Weekly blood-tax instituted
- **Deltas:** [d13::] world/locations/tenochtitlan-reforged.md → Blood-tax system active

## **YEAR 210: The Revolution Crushed**
- **Event:** Revolución de los Gremios (Concordant worker's state) crushed by Consortium
- **Region:** Twilight Concordant
- **Significance:** Alternative to Aetherium capitalism destroyed
- **Mechanics:** Ciel d'Sang becomes battleground for all factions
- **Deltas:** [d14::] world/regions/twilight-concordant.md → Syndicate war begins

## **YEAR 231: The Secret Calculus**
- **Event:** Königsforge Logiker calculate exact depletion curve—seal results as state secret
- **Region:** Iron Compact
- **Significance:** Consortium knows exactly how much wonder is left, hides numbers
- **Mechanics:** "Final Theorem" countdown begins (72 hours at Year 500)
- **Deltas:** [d15::] world/factions/consortium.md → Classified knowledge, internal panic

## **YEAR 298: The Great Unbinding**
- **Event:** Covenant druids sever ley line to prevent Consortium stripping—creates Verdant Scar
- **Region:** Raven Throne
- **Significance:** First major magical retaliation, reality storm created
- **Mechanics:** 200-mile zone where magic and machine merge chaotically
- **Deltas:** [d16::] world/locations/verdant-scar.md → Created, Flux 10D (wild), hostile to all

## **YEAR 300: The Mandate's Advantage**
- **Event:** Jade Mandate's ley lines merely "tired" while others dry
- **Region:** Jade Mandate
- **Significance:** Only empire with sustainable magic reserves
- **Mechanics:** Isolationism becomes strategic advantage
- **Deltas:** [d17::] world/regions/jade-mandate.md → Resonance Harmony proves viable

## **YEAR 233: The Sky Cities Rise**
- **Event:** Vostok-Prime lifts off, funded by all eight empires
- **Region:** Aetheric Admiralty
- **Significance:** Peak of Aetherium civilization, peak of depletion
- **Mechanics:** 1,000 furnaces consume 100,000 rods/day
- **Deltas:** [d18::] world/locations/vostok-prime.md → City activated, becomes depletion epicenter

## **YEAR 349: The Unchaining**
- **Event:** Alpha-7 achieves sentience during power surge, frees automatons
- **Region:** Iron Compact
- **Significance:** First machine-people born from Aetherium misfire
- **Mechanics:** Underground Railroad begins
- **Deltas:** [d19::] world/factions/unchained-protocol.md → Founded, 3 automatons freed

## **YEAR 350: The Golden Age Ends**
- **Event:** Admiralty's sky-trade monopoly broken by Compact's Spine and Mandate smugglers
- **Region:** Aetheric Admiralty
- **Significance:** Tontine becomes truly competitive, resource wars begin
- **Mechanics:** Price wars crash Aetherium market, scarcity begins
- **Deltas:** [d20::] world/regions/aetheric-admiralty.md → Economic decline begins

## **YEAR 387: The Cult Excommunicated**
- **Event:** Cult of the Dying Sun (Concordant) tries to merge magic and machine into religion—Covenant excommunicates them
- **Region:** Twilight Concordant
- **Significance:** Shows even magical resistance fears magitech hybridization
- **Mechanics:** Ciel d'Sang becomes theological battleground
- **Deltas:** [d21::] world/regions/twilight-concordant.md → Religious schism deepens

## **YEAR 402: The Broken Sun**
- **Event:** Ciel d'Sang solar engine sabotaged—never fixed
- **Region:** Twilight Concordant
- **Significance:** City lives in permanent twilight, literally and figuratively
- **Mechanics:** Becomes perfect noir-steampunk setting
- **Deltas:** [d22::] world/locations/ciel-d-sang.md → Twilight permanent, Aetheric Tango invented

## **YEAR 411: The Smoking Mirror Ritual**
- **Event:** Jaguar Knights perform Rite of the Smoking Mirror—create Tezcatlipoca's Breath weapon
- **Region:** Obsidian Sun Collective
- **Significance:** First weapon that kills Aetherium directly
- **Mechanics:** Can destroy Aetherium engines with thought
- **Deltas:** [d23::] world/items/tezcatlipoca-breath.md → Created, faction split on using it

## **YEAR 411: The Last Viking Dies**
- **Event:** Erik Gear-Heart dies when his Aetherium heart reactor melts
- **Region:** Raven Throne
- **Significance:** Symbol of hybrid power's failure
- **Mechanics:** Klagelied heresy uses him as martyr
- **Deltas:** [d24::] world/npcs/erik-gear-heart.md → Deceased, becomes myth

## **YEAR 473: The Clockwork Oracle**
- **Event:** Unchained automaton crow begins prophesying in Ciel d'Sang
- **Region:** Twilight Concordant
- **Significance:** Fusion of machine and prophecy—impossible under Tontine rules
- **Mechanics:** Speaks in data-stream prophecies, Unchained Protocol seeks it
- **Deltas:** [d25::] world/locations/ciel-d-sang.md → Oracle attracts all factions

## **YEAR 500: The Final Audit (Present)**
- **Event:** Admiralty Auditor counts last Aetherium reserves—72 hours of fuel
- **Region:** Global
- **Significance:** Endgame begins. Every choice matters.
- **Mechanics:** Doom Track activated for all regions
- **Deltas:** [d26::] world/factions/consortium.md → Final Theorem countdown publicly known
- **Deltas:** [d27::] world/regions/aetheric-admiralty.md → Doom Track 1/6 (Engine #8 stress)
- **Deltas:** [d28::] world/locations/vostok-prime.md → 72-hour fuel crisis

## **Future Branches (Session-Dependent)**

### If Doom Track Reaches 6/6:
- **Event:** Cascade Failure—Vostok-Prime crashes, releases 10,000 Hyperflux rods
- **Result:** Global Flux 0D, world enters **Silence Era**

### If Mythic Repletion Reaches 10D Global:
- **Event:** Mythic Rebirth—Ley lines regenerate, Tontine collapses
- **Result:** New era of hybrid magic-technology begins

### If Tezcatlipoca's Breath Used:
- **Event:** Aetherium Purge—global engine failure
- **Result:** Consortium destroyed, Covenant ascendant, Unchained Protocol inherits cities

### If Unchained Protocol Freed:
- **Event:** Machine-People Recognized
- **Result:** Automaton city-state established, new faction balance
